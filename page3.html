6 Best Places in the Philippines

The Philippines is well known for its beautiful places that consists of 7,640 islands and filled with historical places built all around this country. Places in the Philipiines are mostly known as the one of the best tourist spots in the world where foreign people always visit. People here are all hospitable and religious. Here are some of the best places in the Philippines that you shoiuld also try to visit.

Mayon Volcano

Mayon Volcano is located in Bicol Albay. It is a popular tourist spot in the Philippines. It has a perfect cone shape that tourists love to visit and admire.

EL NIDO PALAWAN

El Nido is locatedn in Palawan. It is well known for its white-sand beaches and beautiful coral reefs.

TUBATTAHA REEF

Tubattaha Reef is a natural park that is located at the south east of Puerto Princesa city Palawan. It is a marine protected area. This also a renowned scuba diving site. It is home to more that 1,200 marine species.

SIARGAO

Siargao is a tear-dropped shape in the Philippine sea. It is located in the South-east of Tacloban. It is also a sandy-white beaches. Siargao is famous for its suurfing spots so many tourist tend to visit this island.

CHOCOLATE HILLS

Chocolate Hills are a group of unusually shape hills located in the middle of the island of Bohol in Philippines. There are about 1,268 Hills but some estimate that it is as high as 1,776 hills. Most hills are between 30 to 50 meters high.

INTRAMUROS

Intramuros was designated a Natyional Historical Landmark in 1951. The place to explore in the Intramuros are Cobblestone streets, Ancient Fortifications, and Beautiful Colonial Era Buildings.
